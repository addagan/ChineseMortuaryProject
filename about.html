Territory Acknowledgement

We would like to acknowledge that this project has been created on the territory of Lekwungen-speaking peoples on whose lands we work and study. Though this project aims to reveal the impact of settler colonialism on Chinese immigrants during the early years of Fort Victoria, Indigenous peoples to this island have been systematically marginalized through this process of settlement.

About the Project

The aim of this project is to understand what can be learned about the lives of Chinese settlers in Canada through the burial practices of Chinese remains in Victoria, B.C. from the Gold Rush Era to the late 1960s
